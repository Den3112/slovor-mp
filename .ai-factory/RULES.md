- Never use 'pkill node' or similar commands that can terminate the agent's own environment in WSL. Use 'fuser -k <port>/tcp' to only kill specific port processes if needed.
- **Antigravity Eye (Browser Subagent) Stability:** If the browser subagent fails with `ECONNREFUSED` on port 9222 or reports `CDP port not responsive`, it means the headless Chrome process died or was incorrectly killed. Restore it immediately by running: `(google-chrome --headless --remote-debugging-port=9222 --no-sandbox > /dev/null 2>&1 &) && sleep 5`. Do NOT attempt parallel executions of the eye when doing this.
